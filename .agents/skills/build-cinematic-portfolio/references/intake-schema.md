# Intake Schema

## Minimum viable inputs

- Target audience and desired opportunities.
- Name, working languages, short positioning, and contact route.
- Three flagship works with role, year, platform/client, short description, and public link or status.
- One approved hero image at 2000 px or wider.
- One approved image per flagship work, or explicit text-first permission.
- One or more visual references plus what the user likes about each.

## Enhancement inputs

- Transparent foreground portrait (PNG/WebP) for layered hero depth.
- Showreel MP4/poster, press links, contextual metrics, archive credits, collaboration names, and social links.
- Separate desktop/mobile crops only when one focal point cannot preserve the subject.

For every claim record confidence and publishability. For every asset record `owned`, `licensed`, `public-link-only`, `needs-permission`, or `do-not-use`.
